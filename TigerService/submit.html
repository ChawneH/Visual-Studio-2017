Students should ensure they have correctly have all hours certified by a Jackson State approved organization. Only the official service form will be accepted for review by community service advisors.

All Jackson State University students must complete 120 hours of service. Transfer students must complete 60 hours of service. Community service advisors may take up to 48 hours to approve service forms.
