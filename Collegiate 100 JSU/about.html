About Collegiate 100 of Jackson State University

History & Preamble

In 1995, the Collegiate 100 was founded on the campus of Prairie View A&M University by Frederick V. Roberts. The Jackson State University Chapter was charted on April 12, 2018 with the charge to be a responsible, productive, and cooperative segment of the local sponsoring 100 Black Men of America, Inc. Chapter and to be an active organization for the JSU community

The Collegiate 100 of JSU adds value to its community by serving as a corps of service volunteers to help carry out the mission of the 100 Black Men of America, Inc.
